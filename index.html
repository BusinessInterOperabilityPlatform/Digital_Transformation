SCM Central â€“ What It Does for MSMEs

SCM Central is designed to digitise daily MSME operations in a simple, practical manner. Each capability focuses on solving a real operational problem faced on the shop floor.

Order to Cash

Tracks customer orders from receipt to production, dispatch, and payment.

MSME Advantage: No missed orders, reliable delivery commitments, and better cash flow control.

Procure to Pay

Calculates material needs based on actual production plans and tracks supplier deliveries.

MSME Advantage: Fewer shortages, lower excess inventory, and reduced manual follow-ups.

Production Planning & Control

Creates daily and weekly production plans and compares planned vs actual output.

MSME Advantage: Improved throughput, better capacity utilisation, and early problem detection.

Quality & Rework Visibility

Records quality checks and tracks defects or rework.

MSME Advantage: Reduced rejection, improved quality consistency, and stronger customer trust.

Single Source of Truth

Brings orders, materials, production, and dispatch information into one clear view.

MSME Advantage: Faster decisions, less dependency on individuals, and better control.

Integration & Industry 4.0 Ready

Integrates with Tally, Zoho, CRM systems, and can connect to machines or IoT devices.

MSME Advantage: No replacement of existing tools and a gradual, low-risk path to advanced digital capabilities.
